About Us

We are the Group 2 Pixelarity of APEC Pateros, class B07-09-S1-2. This group was established on 2022, consisting 5 members which includes the following members: the facilitator, Hilary Clare David, the scribe, Mischa Gabrielle Abarintos, the spokespeople, Zoe Elisha Blanca and Sharico Baga, and last but definitely not the least, the researcher, Jelce Indedanio. We hope you enjoy our content and bring entertainment along the way! :)

What is our content?

Our content focuses mainly on video podcasting with its aim to inform and raise awareness to people while also remaining a safe space for people to talk about their opinions free of judgment. We value the opinions of others as it helps us gain new perspectives that we may not have thought of around us and teaches not only the viewers, but also us content creators, and maybe help us change for the better.
